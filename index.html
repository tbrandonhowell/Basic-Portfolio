About Me

Let's put some happy little bushes on the other side now. This is the fun part Let's build an almighty mountain. I guess that would be considered a UFO. A big cotton ball in the sky. What the devil.

Trees grow in all kinds of ways. They're not all perfectly straight. Not every limb is perfect. A thin paint will stick to a thick paint. We'll lay all these little funky little things in there. A fan brush is a fantastic piece of equipment. Use it. Make friends with it. Give him a friend, we forget the trees get lonely too.

This is the time to get out all your flustrations, much better than kicking the dog around the house or taking it out on your spouse. I really believe that if you practice enough you could paint the 'Mona Lisa' with a two-inch brush. When things happen - enjoy them. They're little gifts. In this world, everything can be happy. Work on one thing at a time. Don't get carried away - we have plenty of time.
